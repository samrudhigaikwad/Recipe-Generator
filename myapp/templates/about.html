About Smart Recipe Generator

Who We Are

Smart Recipe Generator is an innovative web application designed to help users discover recipes based on available ingredients. Whether you're looking to minimize food waste or try something new, our platform provides intelligent recipe suggestions tailored to your kitchen inventory.

Our Mission
